Probolinggo's Tourist Attractions

Some popular tourist attractions in Probolinggo includes the famous Mount Bromo, and the less famous but still widely-known Candi Jabung

Mount Bromo is one of the most iconic landmarks in Probolinggo and a must-visit destination in East Java. This active volcano is famous for its dramatic sunrise views, which cast golden light over a vast sea of volcanic sand. Visitors can explore the surrounding Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park, hike to the crater, and witness the annual festival where the Tenggerese people offer food, livestock, and flowers into the volcano as a form of gratitude and devotion. The combination of natural beauty and unique cultural traditions makes Mount Bromo an unforgettable experience for tourists and photographers alike

Candi Jabung, on the other hand, offers a window into Indonesia's rich historical and cultural heritage. This 14th-century red-brick Hindu-Buddhist temple dates back to the Majapahit Kingdom and is known for its intricate carvings and symbolic reliefs. Though lesser-known than Mount Bromo, Candi Jabung attracts history enthusiasts and travelers interested in ancient architecture and spirituality. The temple also provides a serene and contemplative atmosphere, making it a fascinating stop for those exploring Probolinggo beyond its natural landscapes
