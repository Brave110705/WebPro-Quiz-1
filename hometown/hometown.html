My Hometown

I was born and raised on Probolinggo Regency, East Java, Indonesia which is known for their tourist spot, Mount Bromo and Candi Jabung. More specifically, i was raised on Paiton Subdistrict which is known for their power plant, which is one of the biggest power plant in Indonesia (And my dad works there!)
