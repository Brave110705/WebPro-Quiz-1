About Me

Hello! My name is Brave Juliada. I am an undergraduate student in Informatics Engineering at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember, Indonesia, currently in my third semester as of Q3 2025

I enjoy game development, especially coding, and creating pixel art for them. Naturally, I also enjoy playing games. In my free time, I usually watch YouTube videos, listen to J-Pop, play games, or work on my game development project, which I hope to release next year

One of the reasons I chose Informatics Engineering as my bachelor's degree is my dream of creating games independently. I began my journey as a game developer in my first semester with Construct 3, which I highly recommend for beginners since it uses blueprints and visual coding, an excellent introduction to game development. Currently, I mainly use Godot for my projects and Aseprite for pixel art. I tried Unity at first, but after trying Godot, I found out that it's faster for 2D games and more pixel-art-friendly

I also want to get into sound engineering, especially for creating game soundtracks for my projects. However, I haven’t found the time for it yet, or, more accurately, I’ve been a bit lazy.
