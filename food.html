Probolinggo's Local Food

Some of the most famous food from Probolinggo are Nasi Glepungan, Nasi Jagung, and while this is a fruit, there's also the Arum Manis Manggo

Nasi Glepungan typically served with various simple but satisfying side dishes like ikan asin (salted fish), tempe, tahu, sambal (chili sauce), and fresh vegetables (lalapan). With its mix of slightly sweet corn, savory rice, salty fish, and spicy sambal, Nasi Glepungan gives a satisfying balance, practical, filling, and a beloved comfort food especially at breakfast

Nasi Jagung literally means "corn rice", but in Probolinggo it's more than just substituting corn for rice. The corn is ground or pounded, then steamed until tender. Giving it a soft, hearty texture.
